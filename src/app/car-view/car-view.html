The Ferrari 288 GTO is a legendary supercar known for its aggressive design and race-inspired performance. Built as Ferrari's first true supercar of the modern era, it features a twin-turbocharged V8 engine that delivers exceptional speed, power, and precision. Originally developed for Group B racing, the 288 GTO combines lightweight construction with advanced aerodynamics, making it both rare and highly desirable.
